Apply to register with {{ practiceName }}

There are two parts for you to complete.

Part 1 Your details

You’ll need to provide:

  • your name and date of birth
  • your address and contact details

You’ll also be asked for:

  • the GP you’re currently registered with if you have one
  • the name and address under which you’re registered with your current GP (if they’re different)
  • your military service number (if you have one) and enlistment date
  • your NHS number if you know it

This is to help {{ practiceName }} find your medical records.

How to find your NHS number

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, for example: 943 476 5919

You can find your NHS number:

  • online
  • on a printed prescription
  • on a letter from your GP or a hospital
  • on your NHS medical card if you have one

Part 2 About your health

You’ll be asked to fill in a health questionnaire which will ask about any allergies, medication and your general health.
